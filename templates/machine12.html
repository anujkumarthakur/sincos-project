Wrap around system

Product Description


This XFC-GB automatic wrap-around packing machine is a 3 in 1 wrap-around case packer consisting of main packer and product feeding system. The product feeding, carton forming, packing in, carton sealing, finished carton discharge all could be done by this machine automatically.

Application


Automatic wrap-around packing machine is widely used in food, daily and chemistry industries, such as milk powder, beer, beverage, instant noodle, detergent, boxed detergent powder, soap, etc.

1.PLC, touch screen and photocell adopt imported unit with UG certificate. Hot melt gluing machine is from American Nordson.


2.Cylinder and valves have the selection of domestic and imported.


3. Touch screen operation is easy and clear, and production and error information both can be displayed on the screen.


4. The wrap-around case packer is equipped with sensors, which alarms and stops when safety door opens or error occurs.


5. Compact design and stainless steel structure is not only reducing the space but also beautiful.
