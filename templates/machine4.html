Carton Erector

Product Description


This machine is used to automatically erect case and seal the bottom of the case with pressure-sensitive adhesive tape like PVC and BOPP. It is used to shape the case of regular shape. Our company can customize case erector according to customers’ requirements to satisfy different needs. The machine has wide applications in the industry of food, chemical fiber, tobacco redrying, pharmacy, publish, air conditioning, household appliance, ceramics, hot work and so on.Our company can drafts the national standard of case erector in China and has years of experience in manufacturing case erector machines. The quality of all materials used and the high technical standards for all mechanical and electronic components combined with highly efficient and precise assembling and testing procedures lead to high performance, safe and durable products..

Machine features:


• The sealing head can be took out easily to equip the new Tape the processing of machine is robust


• This case erector is suitable for a lot of different carton size Adjustment can be finished by handle Alarm, if the carton magazine is empty


• High efficiency and energy saving


• Easy maintenance
